# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and productive environment for everyone. Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical merit
- Accept constructive feedback
- Act in the best interest of the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, and disruptive conduct.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@zlar.ai**. All reports will be reviewed and responded to within 7 days. The project maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public space where an individual represents the project.
